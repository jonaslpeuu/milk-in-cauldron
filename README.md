## âœ¨ Features

- **Pour milk into cauldrons** - Right-click an empty cauldron with a milk bucket to fill it with milk
- **Retrieve milk** - Right-click a full milk cauldron with an empty bucket to get your milk back
- **Visual feedback** - The cauldron displays white milk inside when filled
- **Sound effects** - Authentic bucket pour/fill sounds

## ğŸ® How to Use

1. **Fill a cauldron with milk:**
   - Hold a Milk Bucket
   - Right-click on an empty Cauldron
   - The cauldron fills with milk and you receive an empty bucket

2. **Empty a milk cauldron:**
   - Hold an empty Bucket
   - Right-click on a full Milk Cauldron
   - You receive a Milk Bucket
