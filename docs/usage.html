To fill an empty cauldron with milk:

  1. Obtain a Milk Bucket by right-clicking a cow with an empty bucket
  2. Find or craft a Cauldron and place it in your world
  3. Hold the Milk Bucket in your hand
  4. Right-click on the empty cauldron
  5. The milk will be poured into the cauldron, and you'll receive an empty bucket

To get milk back from a milk cauldron:

  1. Hold an Empty Bucket in your hand
  2. Find a Milk Cauldron (must be full)
  3. Right-click on the milk cauldron
  4. You'll receive a Milk Bucket, and the cauldron becomes empty
